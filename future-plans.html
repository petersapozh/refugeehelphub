short-term goals and Dates

  • We aim to make reviews of at least 9 different organisations and governmnet projects. This will be done by 30 DECEMBER 2023

Our Longterm Goals

The goal with this site ultimately is to keep expanding the scope of the help we can offer. While at the moment we may only be focused on refugees from Ukraine our aim is to expand to a point where will can offer as much help as possible for any type of refugee. The first priority will be adapting the site to be able to help refugees from countries where currently the most international refugees originate such as Syria, Venezuela, Palestine, Afganistan and more. This means that we will need to be updating the site constantly to be able to adapt the site to new crises all over the world. We also want to make sections for the refugee process on all countries that harbour over 100,000 refugees.
